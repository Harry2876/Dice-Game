Objective: The objective of the game is to be the first player to reach a predefined total score, typically 100 points.

Players: The game is played between two players taking turns.

Game Setup: Each player starts with a score of 0.

Gameplay:

Players take turns to roll a six-sided dice, The number rolled on the dice is added to the player's current score.

The player can roll the dice multiple times in a turn to accumulate points, but if they roll a 1, they lose all the points gained in that turn,and it becomes the next player's turn.

The player has the option to "hold," which means they choose to end their turn and add their current score to their main score. It then becomes the next player's turn.

The first player to reach or exceed the predefined total score wins the game.
Alternatively, the game can continue until one player reaches the target score.
